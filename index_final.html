Franchise Process

1

Application & Consultation

Submit your inquiry via our form. Our team will contact you for an initial consultation regarding your desired location and investment plan.

2

Location Analysis

We provide comprehensive commercial area analysis and help you secure the optimal store location using our data-driven system.

3

Contract & Interior

Sign the official franchise agreement. We begin interior construction adhering strictly to Sinjeon's global brand identity.

4

Training & Grand Opening

Complete our rigorous training program covering operations, hygiene, and recipe mastery. Celebrate your Grand Opening with our dedicated support team!
